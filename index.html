About the Founder

Pulkit Vishnoi is a young and passionate entrepreneur with a vision to empower small businesses and freelancers with smart, simple, and reliable digital tools. Coming from a humble background, Pulkit has always believed in building something meaningful—something that truly helps people.

With no formal technical background, he single-handedly built InvoiceGenie—a sleek, user-friendly invoicing and document utility platform designed to make billing effortless and professional for everyone.

Driven by a self-taught hustle mindset, Pulkit turned his passion into purpose, proving that with determination and creativity, even the biggest dreams can be achieved without a traditional path.

His mission is clear: to make powerful digital tools accessible to every corner of India and beyond, enabling individuals to save time, grow faster, and focus on what they love.

For Pulkit, InvoiceGenie isn’t just a product—it’s a purpose. A dream born from the desire to help others simplify their journey and succeed in their own ventures.
